Contoso Minerals · M365 Copilot Legal Immersion

A practical, 100-minute hands-on immersion designed specifically for the Corporate Legal Division of an Indonesian mining holding. We move from Free Copilot Chat → Paid M365 Copilot → Cowork finale → Bonus: Agent Builder. No Copilot Studio required — every capability runs on out-of-the-box M365 Copilot.

About this workshop

Built for the specific reality of Contoso Minerals Legal: a state-owned mining holding with multiple Anak Holding (AH), regulated by UU Minerba, with active litigation, NDA traffic, and Director-level reporting needs.

What you will leave with

  • Fluency with Free Copilot Chat for fast regulatory research (Permen ESDM, Hukumonline, JDIH)
  • A complete Legal Memo drafted in Word grounded on the internal Legal Knowledge Base
  • A marked-up NDA with negotiation cover email — produced in 15 minutes
  • A working litigation analytics view built with the new Excel Copilot Edit + Plan features
  • An Executive Legal Dashboard (self-contained HTML) delivered by Cowork for the Direktur Utama

What this workshop is not

  • It is not a Copilot Studio agent-building session — every exercise uses paid M365 Copilot out-of-the-box
  • It is not a substitute for legal judgement: Copilot drafts, the Legal Counsel decides
  • It is not connected to live Contoso systems today: all data is sample / anonymised

The StoryOne afternoon. Five moments. From regulation to executive dashboard.

Ratna Sari, VP Corporate Legal of Contoso Minerals (Persero), opens her laptop after lunch. A new Permen ESDM has just tightened hilirisasi rules and raised nickel royalty. She has one afternoon to brief the Direktur Utama:

  1. Research the new regulation — using Free Copilot Chat.
  2. Draft a Legal Memo in Word, grounded on the internal Legal Knowledge Base.
  3. Red-flag a counterparty NDA against the M&A Playbook and send a mark-up.
  4. Analyse litigation exposure across all 6 Anak Holding using Excel Plan + Analyst.
  5. Delegate to Cowork to produce an Executive Legal Dashboard (HTML) for the Direktur Utama.
The value: One VP Legal. One afternoon. Research → draft → review → analyse → deliver an executive dashboard. All inside Microsoft 365. No custom agent required.

Exercise 1 · Regulatory Research (Free)10 min · Copilot Chat (Web)

Tier: FREE — Anyone signed in with a Microsoft account. No paid SKU needed. Great way to onboard your Basic-license colleagues.

Use the free, web-grounded Copilot Chat to research the latest changes to Indonesian mining regulation that affect Contoso's nickel and bauxite subsidiaries.

Task 1.14 min☁️ Copilot Chat · Web mode

Research the new Permen ESDM

Open copilot.microsoft.com. Make sure you're in Web mode (not Work). Paste this prompt:

Riset perubahan terbaru atas Permen ESDM No. 11/2018 mengenai kewajiban pengolahan dan pemurnian (hilirisasi) bagi pemegang IUP-OP nikel di Indonesia. Cakup: perubahan tenggat penyelesaian smelter, penyesuaian tarif royalti ekspor bijih nikel, kewajiban pelaporan divestasi bagi pemegang PMA, serta ambang batas jaminan reklamasi. Sertakan sitasi sumber resmi (Kementerian ESDM, JDIH, Hukumonline) dan tautan ke siaran pers resmi jika ada.
What to notice: Copilot Chat surfaces named regulations (UU 3/2020, PP 96/2021), and cites Hukumonline and JDIH — exactly the sources Indonesian legal teams already trust.
Task 1.23 min☁️ Copilot Chat · Web mode

Synthesise impact for an Indonesian mining holding

Continue the same conversation. Ask for synthesis:

Berdasarkan riset di atas, ringkas dalam 5 poin dampak operasional, finansial, dan hukum bagi BUMN holding pertambangan Indonesia yang anak usaha nikel dan bauksitnya BELUM mengoperasikan smelter hilir. Sebutkan kebutuhan top-up jaminan reklamasi dan potensi pemicu Material Adverse Change pada pinjaman sindikasi USD 450 juta.
Task 1.33 min☁️ Copilot Chat · Web mode

Director-level talking points

Ubah poin-poin dampak di atas menjadi talking points 60 detik untuk briefing lisan kepada Direktur Operasi. Sertakan 3 pertanyaan yang kemungkinan akan diajukan Direktur, dengan jawaban 1 kalimat untuk masing-masing.
Caveat: Free Copilot Chat reads the open web. It cannot see your internal Legal KB or contracts. That's what Exercise 2 unlocks.

/01_Contoso_Minerals_Mining_Regulation_Brief.docx /02_Contoso_Minerals_Legal_Knowledge_Base.docx — Berdasarkan kedua dokumen ini, identifikasi 3 isu hukum paling kritis yang dihadapi anak usaha nikel dan bauksit Contoso Minerals di bawah Permen ESDM No. 7 Tahun 2026. Untuk setiap isu, sebutkan: (a) pasal spesifik yang terdampak, (b) referensi SOP atau kebijakan internal kita, dan (c) yurisprudensi Mahkamah Agung relevan dari Legal Knowledge Base.
Task 2.27 min📝 Word Copilot · in-app

Draft the Legal Memo in Word

Open 02 Legal_Memo_Template.docx in Word on the Web. Open the Copilot pane:

Gunakan struktur template Legal Memo dalam dokumen ini, dan hasil analisis dari Copilot Chat tadi, untuk menyusun Legal Memo lengkap yang ditujukan kepada Direktur Utama PT Contoso Minerals (Persero). Isi seluruh 6 bagian: I. Latar Belakang II. Pertanyaan Hukum III. Analisis Hukum (IRAC) IV. Kesimpulan V. Rekomendasi & Mitigasi Risiko VI. Limitasi & Disclaimer Gunakan bahasa hukum Indonesia yang formal. Referensikan Permen ESDM 7/2026, UU 3/2020 (Minerba), PP 96/2021, PP 25/2024, serta Putusan MA No. 2447 K/Pdt/2021 bila relevan. Maksimum 1,5 halaman.
Task 2.33 min📝 Word Copilot · in-app

Add a risk register

Tambahkan tabel risk register pada Bagian V dengan kolom: Risk ID | Deskripsi Risiko | Severity (Tinggi/Sedang/Rendah) | Owner | Mitigasi | Tenggat. Sertakan minimal 6 risiko yang secara spesifik terkait dengan tenggat smelter nikel, opsi tolling bauksit, top-up reklamasi, pemicu MAC, pelaporan divestasi, serta kenaikan royalti.

Exercise 3 · NDA Red-Flag Review (Paid)15 min · Word + Chat (Work)

Tier: PAID — M365 Copilot · Cross-document comparison: counterparty NDA vs your M&A Playbook.
📎 Files: 03 NDA_Draft.docx · 03 MA_Playbook.docx
Task 3.15 min☁️ Copilot Chat · Work mode

Red-flag scan across two documents

/03_Contoso_Minerals_NDA_Draft.docx /03_Contoso_Minerals_MA_Playbook.docx — Bandingkan NDA counterparty dengan M&A Playbook v4.0 kita. Daftarkan setiap klausul yang melanggar standar kita. Untuk setiap pelanggaran, hasilkan satu baris tabel: Klausul # | Isu | Alasan melanggar Playbook | Usulan mark-up (1–2 kalimat).
Expected red flags: Singapore governing law, SIAC arbitration, 7-year term, uncapped indemnity for consequential damages, exclusivity inside the NDA, unilateral assignment, no injunctive-relief bond.
Task 3.27 min📝 Word Copilot · in-app

Generate the mark-up in Word

Open 03 NDA_Draft.docx in Word. Open the Copilot pane:

Tulis ulang Klausul 3 (Jangka Waktu), 5 (Ganti Rugi/Indemnity), 6 (Hukum yang Berlaku & Penyelesaian Sengketa), 7 (Eksklusivitas), dan 9 (Pengalihan) dari NDA ini agar sepenuhnya patuh pada M&A Playbook v4.0 Contoso Minerals. Tampilkan klausul baru dalam gaya mark-up — penambahan dicetak tebal, penghapusan dicoret. Bagian lain dokumen tetap utuh. Sesuaikan dengan gaya bahasa hukum yang sudah dipakai di dokumen.
Task 3.33 min☁️ Copilot Chat · Work mode

Draft the negotiation cover email

Susun email pengantar yang sopan namun tegas kepada Pacific Resources Holdings Pte. Ltd. yang merangkum 5 amandemen utama kita terhadap NDA mereka. Untuk setiap amandemen, berikan justifikasi 1 baris dengan referensi hukum Indonesia bila relevan (KUHPerdata Pasal 1247–1248 tentang ganti rugi; POJK 17/2020 tentang transaksi material; UU PT 40/2007). Usulkan call 30 menit dalam 5 hari kerja. Tanda tangan: Ratna Sari, VP Corporate Legal, PT Contoso Minerals (Persero). Sediakan versi Bahasa Indonesia dan English.

Exercise 4 · Litigation Analytics (Paid)15 min · Excel Edit + Plan + Analyst

Tier: PAID — M365 Copilot · The newest Excel Copilot Edit and Plan modes — no formulas required.
📎 File: 04 Litigation_Cases.xlsx · 50 realistic Indonesian cases across 6 subsidiaries
Task 4.15 min📊 Excel Copilot · Edit

Profile the dataset (Edit)

Open 04 Litigation_Cases.xlsx in Excel for the Web. Open Copilot and switch to Edit:

Profilkan sheet Litigation_Cases. Tambahkan sheet baru bernama "Summary" yang berisi: total kasus, ongoing vs closed, breakdown per Anak_Holding, breakdown per Case_Type, total exposure klaim (IDR Bn), total settlement dibayarkan (IDR Bn), dan win rate keseluruhan. Gunakan header yang rapi dan terapkan conditional formatting (hijau ≥ 60%, kuning 40–60%, merah < 40%) pada kolom win rate.
Task 4.25 min📊 Excel Copilot · Plan

Generate & execute an analytics plan (Plan)

Switch Copilot in Excel to Plan mode. Plan mode proposes a multi-step workflow and asks for your OK before each step.

Susun dan eksekusi workflow analitik berikut, dan konfirmasi dengan saya sebelum menjalankan setiap langkah: 1) Hitung rata-rata durasi (hari) per Case_Type dan per Forum_Level. 2) Identifikasi 5 kasus ongoing high-value teratas berdasarkan Claim_Amount_IDR_Bn. 3) Hitung win/loss rate per Anak_Holding (Menang / total closed). 4) Tandai setiap kasus dengan Duration_Days > 730 sebagai "Aging" di kolom baru. 5) Sisipkan bar chart total exposure (jumlah Claim_Amount_IDR_Bn) per Province.
Task 4.35 min📈 Analyst Agent

Deep-dive root causes with Analyst

In M365 Copilot, open the Analyst agent. Attach the same file.

Dengan menggunakan dataset litigasi, identifikasi 3 akar penyebab teratas yang memicu exposure finansial tertinggi di seluruh 6 Anak Holding. Untuk setiap akar penyebab, hitung: total claim amount, rata-rata durasi, dan rasio menang/kalah. Kemudian rekomendasikan 2 tindakan hukum preventif per akar penyebab, dengan landasan regulasi Indonesia (sitasi UU/PP/Permen).

Task 5.215 min🚀 Cowork

Build the Executive Legal Dashboard (HTML) — ONE prompt

In M365 Copilot, open Cowork. Issue ONE multi-step instruction. Cowork plans the steps, checks in at checkpoints, and executes.

Buatkan saya "Executive Legal Dashboard" untuk Direktur Utama PT Contoso Minerals (Persero). Susun rencana langkah-langkahnya, konfirmasi dengan saya sebelum setiap langkah, lalu eksekusi: 1) Gunakan Researcher untuk menarik status terbaru Permen ESDM No. 7 Tahun 2026 dan setiap surat edaran atau keputusan dirjen terkait. 2) Gunakan Analyst agent pada /04_Contoso_Minerals_Litigation_Cases.xlsx untuk menghitung: total exposure (IDR Bn), win rate keseluruhan, 5 kasus ongoing high-value teratas, breakdown per Case_Type, dan tren 12 bulan kasus baru. 3) Dari notulen yang dihasilkan di Task 5.1 (atau langsung dari /05_Contoso_Minerals_Legal_Sync_Transcript.docx) ekstrak 7 action items terbuka beserta owner dan deadline. 4) Hasilkan satu halaman HTML self-contained berjudul "Contoso Minerals — Executive Legal Dashboard — Mei 2026". Sertakan: 4 KPI tile di atas (Total Exposure, Win Rate, Ongoing Cases, Open Actions), tabel Top-5 ongoing cases, bar chart exposure per Case_Type, panel Regulatory Watchlist dengan status Permen ESDM 7/2026, serta daftar Open Action Items. Gunakan tema korporat navy gelap + putih, font Inter/Segoe UI. Inline seluruh CSS dan SVG chart — tanpa dependensi eksternal. 5) Simpan HTML sebagai ContosoMinerals_Legal_Dashboard.html di OneDrive saya.
What to watch for: Cowork will pause at each step and show you the plan, the data it found, and a draft. Approve or redirect at every checkpoint — that's the point.
Task 5.33 min📊 PowerPoint Copilot

Turn the dashboard into a board-ready deck for the Direktur Utama

Slide: (1) Cover dengan tanggal dan "Confidential — Board Use Only", (2) Permen ESDM 7/2026 — 3 perubahan utama dan status kepatuhan kita, (3) 5 litigasi ongoing high-value teratas dengan exposure dan forum, (4) Risiko terbesar dan usulan mitigasinya, (5) Asks dan keputusan yang dibutuhkan dari BOD. Task 6.15 min🤖 M365 Copilot · Agent Builder

Create the agent — describe it in natural language

Open M365 Copilot. Click + Create agent (or open Agent Builder from the side panel). On the "Describe" tab, paste:

Create a new agent called "Contoso Legal Counsel". Description: Internal legal assistant for the Corporate Legal Division of PT Contoso Minerals (Persero), grounded on Indonesian mining regulation and our internal Legal Knowledge Base. Instructions: - Always answer in Bahasa Indonesia unless the question is in English. - Cite the specific UU, PP, Permen ESDM, POJK, KUHPerdata article, or Putusan Mahkamah Agung when giving legal answers. - Use the IRAC structure (Issue–Rule–Application–Conclusion) for any legal opinion. - If the question requires updated regulation outside the attached knowledge base, say so and recommend escalation to the VP Corporate Legal. - Never give definitive advice on litigation strategy without flagging that internal counsel review is required. Tips & Reminders

  • Be specific — name the format, length, audience, and tone.
  • Always cite Indonesian sources when asking Copilot to research regulation: "Cite Kementerian ESDM, JDIH, Hukumonline".
  • Use / to reference files in Copilot Chat (Work) — it's the difference between generic answers and grounded answers.
  • For Excel, prefer Plan mode for multi-step analytics — it shows you the plan before touching your data.
  • Cowork checkpoints are your safety net — review every plan before approving.
  • Agent Builder ≠ Copilot Studio — Agent Builder is the no-code option inside M365 Copilot, included with your paid licence; Copilot Studio is a separate product.
  • Never paste real PII or commercially sensitive data in a workshop. UU PDP applies.
Licensing reminder: Exercise 1 works for everyone with a Microsoft account (Free). Exercises 2–5 require a paid M365 Copilot licence. For your Basic-licence colleagues, encourage daily use of Free Copilot Chat for research, summarisation, and drafting.
